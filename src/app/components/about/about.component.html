About Us

The Hall of Residence 3 is one of the oldest boys’ hostels of IIT Kanpur. The hostel comprises of 7 blocks, each of which is a three-storey building with 10-14 adjacent rooms on each floor in which 1st, 2nd and 3rd year U. G. students reside.

The unique culture of the hall ensures that a student not only flourishes in his studies but also develops great command in other spheres of life. The healthy interaction among juniors and seniors during times of co-curricular activities develops a great sense of bonding and trust among all.

Although the Hall-3 Mess and Canteen have always been greatly praised but you can always find different snacks in the evening right outside the hall gate!

We welcome you to The Hall of Residence 3, a place where the journey of success begins…
