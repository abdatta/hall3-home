Ex-Hall Executive Committee

The Ex-Hall Executive Committee (Ex-HEC) represents a cohort of former student leaders who previously served on the Hall Executive Committee (HEC), overseeing the governance and management of the hall. Their tenure on the HEC equipped them with invaluable experience and insights into the intricacies of hall affairs, ranging from policy formulation to infrastructure development and resident welfare. Their contribution to the Hall community remains palpable. Through their collective expertise and dedication, the Ex-HEC continues to play a vital role in preserving institutional knowledge, providing mentorship to current HEC members, and offering guidance on critical decisions impacting the hall's well-being. Their appraisal of their contribution to the hall is characterized by a deep sense of pride and fulfillment, knowing that their efforts have helped shape the hall's culture, facilities, and overall environment for the better. While their official responsibilities may have concluded, their commitment to the hall's continued success endures, as they remain steadfast allies and advocates for the hall community's prosperity and growth.
